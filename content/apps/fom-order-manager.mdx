## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you register and use Order Manager, we collect:

- **Account information** — your name, shop name, email address, and phone number
- **Shop details** — shop category, Facebook Page link, and township/location
- **Order data** — customer names, phone numbers, delivery addresses, product names, quantities, prices, and order statuses that you manually enter
- **Customer data** — information about your customers that you add to the app
- **Payment screenshots** — when you submit proof of payment for your subscription

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the Service, we automatically collect:

- **Device information** — device type, operating system, and browser type
- **Usage data** — features you use, pages you visit, and actions you take within the app
- **Log data** — IP address, access timestamps, and error logs
- **App performance data** — crash reports and performance metrics

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do **not**:

- Access your Facebook Messenger conversations
- Collect data directly from Facebook or any third-party social platform
- Read, store, or process messages between you and your customers
- Collect payment card numbers or banking credentials

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide the Service** — create and maintain your account, store your orders and customer records
- **Improve the Service** — understand how the app is used and identify areas for improvement
- **Send notifications** — order reminders, daily summaries, and feature updates (you can opt out)
- **Process subscriptions** — verify payments and activate or renew your plan
- **Provide support** — respond to your questions and resolve issues
- **Ensure security** — detect fraud, prevent abuse, and protect user data
- **Legal compliance** — meet our obligations under applicable Myanmar law

We do **not** sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third parties for marketing purposes.

## 4. How We Store Your Data

### 4.1 Storage Location

Your data is stored securely on cloud servers. We use **Firebase (Google Cloud)** infrastructure for data storage and real-time sync. Data may be stored on servers located outside Myanmar, including in Singapore and the United States, but is governed by this Privacy Policy regardless of location.

### 4.2 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Firebase Authentication for secure login
- Role-based access controls — only you can access your shop's data
- Regular security reviews

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

### 4.3 Data Retention

- **Active accounts:** We retain your data for as long as your account is active
- **Cancelled accounts:** We retain your data for **30 days** after cancellation, giving you time to export
- **Deleted accounts:** Upon account deletion, your data is permanently erased within **7 business days**
- **Payment records:** We retain transaction records for up to 3 years for legal and accounting purposes

## 5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your data. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

### 5.1 Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party providers who help us operate the Service:

| Provider         | Purpose                           |
| ---------------- | --------------------------------- |
| Google Firebase  | Database, authentication, hosting |
| Google Analytics | App usage analytics (anonymised)  |
| Facebook SDK     | Optional Facebook login           |

All service providers are contractually required to protect your data and may only use it to provide services to us.

### 5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests by authorities in Myanmar or other jurisdictions.

### 5.3 Business Transfers

If Order Manager is acquired or merged with another company, your data may be transferred. We will notify you before this happens and give you the option to delete your account.

### 5.4 Government and Law Enforcement Requests

We may disclose user data in response to valid legal requests from public authorities, including:

- Court orders
- Subpoenas
- Warrants
- Other lawful legal process

**Our policies for handling such requests:**

1. **Legality Review**: We review all requests for legal validity before disclosing any data. We require proper legal process issued to "Aetheria Tech Co., Ltd." (the legal entity operating Order Manager).

2. **Data Minimization**: We disclose only the minimum information necessary to comply with the legal requirement. We do not voluntarily provide more data than legally obligated.

3. **Challenging Unlawful Requests**: If we believe a request is overly broad, unlawful, or invalid, we will challenge it to the extent legally permitted. We may seek to narrow the scope or oppose the request entirely.

4. **User Notification**: When permitted by law, we will notify affected users before disclosing their data, giving them an opportunity to challenge the request.

5. **Documentation**: We maintain records of all government requests received, including:
   - Date of request
   - Requesting authority
   - Legal basis cited
   - Data requested
   - Data disclosed (if any)
   - Our response and legal reasoning

**Important**: We do not provide bulk access to user data. We do not cooperate with mass surveillance programs. We respond only to targeted, lawful requests for specific user accounts.

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

### 6.1 Access

You can view all the data stored in your account at any time from within the app.

### 6.2 Correction

You can update your shop profile, account details, and any order or customer data directly in the app.

### 6.3 Data Export

You can export all your orders and customer data as an Excel file from **Settings → Export All Data** at any time.

### 6.4 Deletion

You can request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data. See our **Account Deletion** page for the step-by-step process.

### 6.5 Notification Preferences

You can opt out of marketing notifications in **Settings → Notifications**. Essential account notifications (payment confirmations, security alerts) cannot be disabled.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: **support@getfom.com** or message us on Facebook.

## 7. Customer Data You Enter

When you enter your customers' information (names, phone numbers, addresses) into Order Manager, you become a **data controller** for that data. This means:

- You are responsible for obtaining your customers' consent to store their information
- You should only enter information that is relevant and necessary for order management
- You must not use the data for purposes beyond managing your shop's orders

We act as a **data processor** on your behalf for this customer data, storing it securely as per this policy.
